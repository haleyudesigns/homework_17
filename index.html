Project Name:

Out of the Closet Thrift Stores Website Redesign

Designers:

Haley Ugwuibe, Ruthie Brown & Charlie Norice

Year:

2019

Overview

Owned by the AIDS Healthcare Foundation nonprofit provider) each Out of the Closet Thrift store contains free HIV testing, and a pharmacy. Out of the Close is a Thrift store that sells clothing, books & housewares. A total of .96 cents of each dollar goes toward AIDS Healthcare Foundation.

Time

This project was completed within a three week time frame.

Problem

Out of the Closet is a non-profit chain of thrift stores whose revenues provide medical care for patients with HIV/ AIDs. However, OTC’s current web design likely doesn’t maximize its foot traffic and donations.

Solution

By creating a website for Out of the Closet Thrift Store, targeted toward donating items and HIV Awareness, we will increase the foot traffic of the OTC website, and positively impact Atlanta's HIV statistic.
